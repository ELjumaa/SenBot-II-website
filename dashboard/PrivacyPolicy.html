Privacy Policy

SenBot-II is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use the Bot.

Information Collection and Use

  • We collect information about you when you interact with the Bot, such as when you send commands or messages to the Bot. This information may include your Discord username, user ID, and any messages or other information you send to the Bot.
  • We use this information to operate, improve, and personalize the Bot. For example, we may use your information to respond to your requests or to provide you with information about the Bot's features.
  • We may also collect information about your server and other users on the server. This information is collected for the purpose of providing better service to the server.

Sharing of Information

  • We will not share any information we collect about you with any third party except as required by law or to protect the safety of our users or the public.
  • We may share aggregate, non-personally identifiable information about our users with third parties for research and analysis purposes.

Data Retention

We retain the information we collect about you for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected and to comply with applicable laws.

Security

  • We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data.
  • We restrict access to your personal information to employees and contractors who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve the Bot.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting the information we collect. Your continued use of the Bot constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any updates.
